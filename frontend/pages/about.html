Our Mission

Connecting Farms to Your Table

FreshMart was founded in 2019 with one simple belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, nutritious, and affordable groceries. We partner directly with local farmers and certified organic producers to cut out the middlemen and bring you the freshest produce at the best prices.

Today, we serve over 50,000 happy households across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, with plans to expand to 20 more cities by 2026.
